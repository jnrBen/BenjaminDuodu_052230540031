WELCOME TO THE
FACULTY OF APPLIED SCIENCE
AND TECHNOLOGY

The Faculty of Applied Science and Technology
at Kumasi Technical University (KsTU)
emphasizes practical learning
and innovation
despite resource limitations.
It nurtures problem-solving
abilities, promotes research
and fosters community engagement to empower
students as agents of change within their societies.
Despite challenges, KTU's faculty remains dedicated
to cultivating a culture of hands-on
education and technological advancement.
Through partnerships with local
industries and organizations,
students gain valuable experiential
learning opportunities
and contribute meaningfully to addressing societal needs.
KTU's commitment to excellence underscores
its role as a catalyst for socio-economic development in the region.

"In the pursuit of knowledge,
we illuminate paths for
future generations.
Education is not solely
about information but
about instilling curiosity and critical thinking.
It's a journey of exploration,
where every question asked is a
step towards enlightenment."
